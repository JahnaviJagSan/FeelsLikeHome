About Us

Feels like Home is a technology based platform for booking PGs by location with specific requirements by filtering by Location,Price, Room Type, Gender, Food etc. Currently, we have launched the Platform in Delhi only. We will soon expand to all major cities of the country.

Our Aim

Our Aim and motto are simple and singular. To provide the guests with a PG that feels like homes best fitting their needs and the hometowners a guest who fits right in. To attain this we work with hometowners and guests to give everyone involved the best possible experience.
